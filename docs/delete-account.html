Delete your learn1000words account

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time. Deletion is immediate and irreversible — there is no grace period, and deleted data cannot be recovered.

What gets deleted: your account (sign-in credentials), your learning profile (nickname, avatar, level, streak, memorized-word count), every word you have memorized, your friend requests and friendships, and all app data stored on your device. This cannot be undone.

If you still have the app installed

Delete your account instantly from within the app:

  1. Open learn1000words and go to the Profile tab.
  2. In the Account section, tap Delete account.
  3. Confirm in the dialog. Your account and all data are deleted right away, and you are returned to the registration screen.

If you no longer have the app

Email us to request deletion:

To: ermellinodavide@gmail.com
Subject: Account deletion request

Send the request from the email address you registered with so we can verify that the account is yours. Once verified, your account and all associated data are deleted immediately.
