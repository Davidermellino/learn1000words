## What the App does

learn1000words helps you build vocabulary in a chosen language pair using flashcards, short
tests, and a written-practice ("Usage") mode, with levels, a daily goal, a streak, an optional
daily reminder notification, and an optional friends feature. Your learning profile and the words
you have memorized are backed up to our cloud backend so they can be restored when you sign in on
another device. Some data — your written practice sentences, your daily goal, your reminder time,
and your daily activity history — stays only on your device. See the
[Privacy Policy](https://learn1000words.com/privacy-policy.html) for full detail on what is stored
and where.
